01 / Background

From a blank Shopify install to a top-3 ranking for the category's core terms.

Eco-Building Products came to OgreLogic in October 2023 with a clear ask: build us a Shopify store that can carry the depth of our catalog without becoming unusable. Sustainable building materials is a long-tail category — every product has variations, certifications, technical specs, and brand context that matters to specifiers and contractors. Generic ecommerce templates collapse under that weight. Ours doesn't.

Oct 2023 — Sep 2024
Phase 01 · Build

Shopify Storefront

Thousands of SKUs, multi-brand catalog, variant-heavy products, custom plugins for category navigation, technical-spec layouts, and merchandising. Launched September 2024.

Mar 2025

Client had a separate marketing partner under contract and was dissatisfied with results. We were brought in for a paid audit. They liked what they saw.

06 / What We Did

Six disciplines, one accountable partner.

The client's previous arrangement was fragmented. Ours is consolidated by design — and the compounding effect across channels is what produced the result above.

01

Shopify Build & Maintenance

Built the storefront from scratch. Catalog architecture for thousands of variant-heavy SKUs across multiple sustainable-product brands.

  • Category nav for technical specifiers
  • Variant logic, certifications, spec sheets
  • Page speed & Core Web Vitals tuning
02

On-Page SEO

The lift that took terms like "green flooring" from position 98 to 6, and "recessed light insulation" to position 1.

  • Meta, OG, schema, alt-tags, sitemap, robots
  • Internal linking & topic clusters
  • Product-page copy rewrites for intent match
03

Off-Page SEO & Content

Ongoing authority-building program — original content, press, classifieds, blogs, articles, and promotion every single month.

  • Article & press release writing + promotion
  • Blog production on technical building topics
  • Search engine + classified submissions
04

Email — Mailchimp Certified Partner

Took over email marketing using OgreLogic's Mailchimp Certified Partner credentials. Lifecycle, segmentation, and revenue from a previously underutilized channel.

  • Abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back
  • Segmented monthly newsletters
  • Returning customer rate now +22% YoY
05

Paid Search & Shopping Support

Google Ads support layered on top of organic — paid shopping contributed 7.9K sessions and a meaningful share of revenue alongside the organic growth.

  • Shopping feed health & PMax structure
  • Budget pacing aligned to SEO gaps
  • $53K Google Ads spend supporting $122K ecommerce
06

Social & GEO Strategy

Social media guidance plus first-mover work on generative-engine optimization for AI search surfaces.

  • Content structured for LLM citability
  • Schema & entity work for AI surfacing
  • AI traffic measurement instrumented end-to-end
